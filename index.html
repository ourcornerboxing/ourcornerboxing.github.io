what we're about

In a boxing ring, your corner is the place you go to reset, where people have your back, where you figure out how to make it through the next round. It is a place where you can rest and gather your focus and determination. Our main purpose here is to create an environment where you can feel at home, develop skills, confidence and strength, and build community.

Boxing is a sport that develops awareness of the body, discipline, strength and emotional regulation. A good foundation in boxing is extremely valuable in other martial arts and for body mechanics in general. Classes and coaching here place a priority on teaching the fundamentals well and with care. If you have always wanted to box but didn't feel welcome in other gyms, if you want to learn skills to help you defend yourself, or if you just want a low key place to work out and make friends, this is a place for you!
