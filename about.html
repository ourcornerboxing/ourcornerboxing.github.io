about the space

We are in the process of fixing up a historic boxing gym and community center to be, well, a boxing gym and community center. It was left in really rough shape and has taken a lot of work to get to where it is now! The pictures on this page will show our current progress, but everything should be set up for classes to start in late July.



We will have 6-8 heavy bags, a free weights area with a pull up bar, treadmills, a row machine, and mats and resistance bands. We also have a bathroom with a shower, and are hoping to install a free community fridge. HEPA air purifiers will be running during all open hours to prevent the spread of airborne pathogens.
